Which hosting plan is best for me?

Starter is ideal for small websites, Business is better for growing sites, and Pro is built for larger websites that need more resources and performance.

How quickly will my hosting be ready?

Most hosting accounts are activated quickly after ordering, so you can log in, connect your domain, upload files, and start building your website.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes. You can start with a smaller package and upgrade later as your website grows, without needing to rebuild your site from scratch.

Do the plans include useful website tools?

Yes. Plans are designed to include practical hosting tools such as cPanel access, NVMe SSD storage, email features, and website management options.

Will my website be secure?

HostMeerkat hosting is built with security, reliable infrastructure, backups, and support in mind to help keep your website protected and online.

What if I need help after ordering?

You can contact support through the client area if you need help with setup, hosting questions, technical issues, or choosing the right next step.

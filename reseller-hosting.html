What is Reseller Hosting?

Reseller hosting allows you to purchase a large chunk of server resources from us and divide it into smaller hosting plans that you can sell to your own clients. You act as the hosting provider!

Is your Reseller Hosting completely white-label?

Yes, 100%. We remain completely invisible to your clients. You can set up custom, branded nameservers (e.g., ns1.yourdomain.com) and add your own logo to your clients' cPanel dashboards.

Do I get WHM (Web Host Manager)?

Yes. All of our reseller hosting plans come with WHM, which gives you master administrative control to create, suspend, and manage your clients' individual cPanel accounts.

Can I upgrade my Reseller plan later?

Absolutely. As your client base grows, you can seamlessly upgrade your reseller plan to a higher tier to increase your cPanel account limits and disk space without any downtime.

Do you handle technical support for my clients?

We provide 24/7 technical support to you (the reseller) regarding server issues, WHM, and infrastructure. You will act as the first line of support for your own clients. If you run into an issue you can't solve, simply open a ticket with us!

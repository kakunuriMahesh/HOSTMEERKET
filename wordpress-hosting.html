Quick Answers

WordPress Questions, Answered

Everything you need to know about getting started with managed WordPress hosting.

Already have a site?

We migrate your existing WordPress site for free — no downtime, no stress.

Request Migration →
What makes this different from regular hosting?

Our WordPress hosting is purpose-built for WP. The server stack (LiteSpeed, PHP 8.3, NVMe SSD, LSCache) is pre-configured for maximum WordPress performance, and we handle core and plugin updates automatically so you can focus on your content.

Will you migrate my existing WordPress site?

Yes — free of charge on all plans. Our team will clone your site, verify it loads correctly, then switch the DNS. You experience zero downtime throughout the process.

Do you support WooCommerce?

Absolutely. All plans are WooCommerce-compatible. The Business WP and Agency WP plans are particularly well-suited thanks to their higher resource limits and LiteSpeed + CDN combo, which keeps shop pages fast even under traffic spikes.

How do backups work?

We run automated daily backups of your full WordPress installation — files and database — with a 30-day retention window. You can restore any snapshot with a single click from your control panel, or contact our team if you need a point-in-time restore.

Can I host multiple WordPress sites on one plan?

Yes. The Business WP plan supports up to 5 sites and the Agency WP plan supports unlimited WordPress installations, each with their own cPanel account and isolated resources.
